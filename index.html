Duo Lineage

  • Dili begat Pao.
  • Pao begat Ti.
  • Ti begat three sons:
    • Chulu (no sons)
    • Laochao (no sons)
    • Kha
  • Kha’s Descendants (Ti’s Son):
    • Kha begat Kholu.
    • Kholu begat Ngaolu.
    • Ngaolu’s Descendants:
      • Yiizii begat Chao.
      • Chao begat two sons:
        • Paoyu
        • Khulu
      • Paoyu’s Descendants (Chao’s son):
        • Paoyu begat Khohro, Kha, Daova, Khochi, and Yudu.
        • Khohro’s Descendants (Paoyu’s Son):
          • Khohro begat Vi.
          • Vi begat Seiba.
          • Seiba begat Ngaolu.
          • Ngaolu begat Dazii.
          • Dazii begat Ruth, Jona, Helenah, Senai, Silina, Poanai, Duo, Esther, and Hoping.
        • Kha’s Descendants (Paoyu’s Son):
          • Kha begat Pao.
          • Pao begat Paoyu.
          • Paoyu begat Anthony and Atho.
        • Daova’s Descendants (Paoyu’s Son):
          • Daova begat Chulu and Khazii.
        • Chulu’s Descendants (Daova’s Son):
          • Chulu begat Khohri.
          • Khohri begat Khochi.
          • Khochi begat Vahrii, Pavei, Lovely, and Mevei.
        • Khazii’s Descendants (Daova’s Son):
          • Khazii begat Nasii.
          • Nasii begat Veikho.
          • Veikho begat Pouveinai.
        • Khochi’s Descendants (Paoyu’s Son):
          • Khochi begat Thaimoso.
          • Thaimoso begat Kha, Seizii, Louni, and Hrailu.
